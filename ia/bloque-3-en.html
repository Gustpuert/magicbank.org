Official Guide to Using MagicBank

How to enter, learn, and make the most of the platform

Before explaining how to learn with MagicBank, it is essential to understand a basic and non-negotiable requirement: to use MagicBank — even for a guided tour — you must have an active ChatGPT session.

This requirement is not technical by whim. It is structural. MagicBank is not an isolated platform. It is a system that is powered by ChatGPT and, at the same time, enhances ChatGPT through an advanced educational layer.

Why an active ChatGPT session is mandatory

MagicBank uses ChatGPT to run its tutors, maintain contextual memory, respond in real time, and adapt instruction to each user.

At the same time, MagicBank enhances ChatGPT through advanced instructions, behavioral prompts, thematic specialization, integration of books and academic knowledge, and proprietary pedagogical models.

If you do not have an active ChatGPT session, informational tutors will not function, guided tours will not display correctly, and demonstrative or final tutors cannot be activated.

What MagicBank is and how it is organized

MagicBank is an educational platform with artificial intelligence instructors designed to surpass the traditional model of generic teachers, recorded courses, and static content.

It does not replicate the classic educational model. It transforms it through specialized tutors, guided instruction, and adaptation to the learner’s real pace.

MagicBank University

MagicBank University is organized by faculties, each with highly specialized tutors. Some of the available faculties include Law, Accounting, Business Administration, Marketing, and Software Development.

Each university tutor teaches a single discipline, maintains thematic coherence, stays constantly updated, and applies high academic rigor.

MagicBank Academy

MagicBank Academy offers practical and applied training. It includes areas such as cooking and gastronomy, intelligent nutrition, and language learning, including English, French, Italian, Portuguese, German, and Mandarin Chinese.

Language tutors teach patiently, correct pronunciation, and accompany users in real contexts such as travel, conferences, and everyday conversations.

Arts and Trades

MagicBank includes training factories designed to teach complete trades, such as ice cream production, international cocktails, desserts, hot and cold beverages, and pizza making.

Each factory allows learning for enjoyment or entrepreneurship, using tutors optimized for real-world processes.

Introduction to the Tutor Factory

MagicBank includes a Tutor Factory dedicated to creating artificial intelligence assistants specialized for real professional and human contexts. This is where the Professional Assistant Tutors (TAP) and MagicBank Supertutors are developed.
